About the Technology Radar

The Research & Prototyping Technology Radar is a list of emerging industry technologies, detailing how much attention and effort our team is devoting to them and how they align with our team's services.

We use four rings with the following semantics:

PROMOTE

Technologies in this ring have been assessed and deemed relevant and valuable to the organization.

ASSESS

Analysis is actively being performed by the team to evaluate the potential of the technology and its relevance to the organization (ETAC, Primers, FAQs, …)

MONITOR

This ring is the entry point for our radar, an it's populated with emerging technologies that have been deemed promising and warrant further observation.

HOLD

Technolgies listed in this ring have been observed by our team and deemed not currently relevant for further action.
